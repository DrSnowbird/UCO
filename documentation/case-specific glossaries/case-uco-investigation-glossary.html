A
AuthorizationFacet

A kind of Facet. A valid occurrence satisfies the following necessary conditions:

  • authorizationType exactly one occurrence of ControlledVocabulary.
  • authorizationIdentifier at least one value of BasicString.

Identifies some form of authorization for investigatory action.

F
ForensicAction

A kind of Action.

A forensic examination action taken as part of a cyber investigation.

I
Investigation

A kind of ContextualCompilation. A valid occurrence satisfies the following necessary condition:

  • investigationForm exactly one occurrence of ControlledVocabulary.

A valid occurrence may also have the following properties:

  • investigationStatus at most one occurrence of ControlledVocabulary.
  • startTime at most one value of Timestamp.
  • endTime at most one value of Timestamp.
  • actionLifecycle_Refs any number of occurrences of ActionLifecycle.
  • provenanceRecordRefs any number of occurrences of ProvenanceRecord.
  • actionRefs any number of occurrences of Action.
  • roleRefs any number of occurrences of Role.

An exploration of the facts involved in a cyber-relevant set of suspicious activity.

P
ProvenanceRecord

A kind of ContextualCompilation. A valid occurrence may have the following properties:

  • exhibitNumber at most one value of BasicString.
  • Refs any number of occurrences of UcoObject.
